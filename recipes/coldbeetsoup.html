Lithuanian Cold Beet Soup

Description

Pink soup is a cold, refreshing pink beetroot soup that is a must when visiting Lithuania!

Ingredients

  • 450g boiled & cooled beetroot straws
  • 100g cucumber
  • 6 spring onions
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1 liter kefir
  • Fresh dill to taste
  • Salt to taste

For the side dish:

  • 200g potatoes
  • Fresh dill

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and chop into bite-sized pieces. Place in a saucepan of boiling water, add a good pinch of salt and boil until a knife can easily pierce the flesh.
  2. While the potatoes are boiling, assemble the soup.
  3. Chop the eggs into small dice.
  4. Chop the scallions or onions leaves into 1 cm pieces.
  5. Finely chop the dill.
  6. Pour the kefir into a large bowl or saucepan and add the chopped ingredients, holding back some of the dill for garnish.
  7. Taste and season salt as required.
  8. Ladle the soup into bowls and sprinkle with the remaining dill.
  9. Serve the potatoes on a side plate so that they do not heat the soup.
